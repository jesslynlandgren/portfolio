Front End Development

As a full-stack developer, I build sites from start to finish. I am proficient in HTML/CSS as well as Bootstrap 3.0 so that I can ensure your site is fully responsive, showing crisp and clean on all phones, tablets, and computers.

Back End Development

UI/UX

My priority when building a site is to your users and creating an experience that is both straightforward and aesthetically pleasing. I also provide advanced design of cartographic and geovisiual elements, including my own custom Mapbox styles

About Me

Lover of all things detailed, colorful, and spatial

I am currently a student in the immersive full-stack development bootcamp at Digital Crafts. I am coming to the world of development via the environmental and earth sciences. After a few early career years as an environmental engineering consultant, I knew I wanted to explore my interests in computing and organizing information. I fell into coding and haven't looked back! In my spare professional time, I play with geographic applications and visualizations as much as possible.

I grew up in North Carolina, and have been in and out of the Atlanta area since. After finishing the Digital Crafts program in early January 2017, I will be looking for a full-time development position in the north Atlanta metro and OTP north areas. In my non-professional spare time, I enjoy swimming, hiking, and netflix binges.
