Front End Development

As a full-stack developer, I build sites from start to finish. I am proficient in HTML/CSS as well as Bootstrap 3.0 so that I can ensure your site is fully responsive, showing crisp and clean on all phones, tablets, and computers.

Back End Development

My skills also allow me to manage a site from the backend, setting up the acess and maintenance of the data your site needs. I develop using the MEAN stack and Python, while specializing in spatial/geographic backend tools such like Mapbox.js and CartoDB.js.

UI/UX

My priority when building a site is to your users and creating an experience that is both straightforward and aesthetically pleasing. I also provide advanced design of cartographic and geovisiual elements, including my own custom Mapbox styles
